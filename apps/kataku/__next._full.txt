d:T866,# Privacy Policy - Kataku

Last updated: 2026

Kataku is an educational application designed to help children learn vocabulary pronunciation through interactive learning activities.

This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles information when used by users, including children.

---

## Information Collection

Kataku does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

The application may request access to the device microphone to enable speech recognition features. This feature allows users to practice vocabulary pronunciation within the app.

Microphone input is used only for the purpose of speech recognition and is not stored, recorded, or transmitted by the application.

---

## Permissions Used

The application may request the following permission:

### Microphone
Used to allow speech recognition so users can practice pronunciation during learning activities.

The application does not record or store voice data.

---

## Third-Party Services

Kataku may use third-party services that may process data according to their own privacy policies. These services may include:

- Google Speech Recognition
- Google Play Services

Please review their privacy policies for more information.

---

## Children's Privacy

Kataku is designed for children as an educational application.

The application does not knowingly collect personal information from children.  
If a parent or guardian believes that a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will promptly remove such information.

---

## Data Security

We value the security of users' information. Since Kataku does not collect personal data, there is minimal risk related to data storage or misuse.

---

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Users are advised to review this page periodically for any updates.
